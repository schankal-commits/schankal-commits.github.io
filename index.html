Bio

Hi! I'm alex, a amateur game developer who is highly passionate about creating entertaining experiences. I'm currently a student at Michigan State University, majoring in games and interactive media. I love anything from playing video games, creating projects, reading, drawing, etc.

Game Development

Why did I choose it?

Game Development

Why game development?

Honestly I can't really remember the first time my interest in game development arose, but it kind of just became something I entertained and taught myself to do after taking an afterschool class on the basics of coding. Since then I've been making games for I think around nine years now.

What have I made?

Over the years I've created hundreds of little games, most unfinished, but a few have actually survied and become fully fledged experiences. I've made a badly designed online multiplayer fighting game, a game jam entry snake puzzle game with a level editor, a website for creating worlds for another website, and a bunch of others!

Merryfear Mall - One of my most recent games!

Ouroboros - A puzzle game I made with my brother for a game jam!
